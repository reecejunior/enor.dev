    ## The liability problem

    The integration of LLMs in clinical settings has introduced significant liability risks:

    - <span className="inline-flex items-center justify-center w-6 h-6 rounded-full bg-red-500/10 text-red-500 text-[11px] font-bold shrink-0 mt-0.5">!</span> **51% of triage cases** — ChatGPT recommended "wait and see" for conditions requiring immediate clinical intervention.
    - <span className="inline-flex items-center justify-center w-6 h-6 rounded-full bg-amber-500/10 text-amber-500 text-[11px] font-bold shrink-0 mt-0.5">!</span> **71% of healthcare workers** admitted to inputting patient data into commercial LLMs, creating HIPAA violations.

    <Callout type="warning" title="Why this matters">
      Without deterministic safety rails, every LLM response in a clinical context is a potential liability event.
    </Callout>

    ---
